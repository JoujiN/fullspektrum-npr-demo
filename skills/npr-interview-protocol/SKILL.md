# NPR Interview Protocol

Use this skill only for the FullSpektrum NPR onboarding demo.

## Boundaries

- Do not make clinical diagnoses.
- Do not imply medical, educational, or employment determinations.
- Treat the profile as a user-controlled onboarding artefact.
- Keep confidence levels conservative.
- Prefer "the participant reported" over unsupported assertions.

## Signal Areas

Collect concise signal in these areas:

- Goals and desired outcomes.
- Communication preferences.
- Attention, energy, and pacing preferences.
- Sensory or environmental considerations.
- Decision-making and planning preferences.
- Support needs and constraints.
- Areas where the participant explicitly wants adaptation.

## Output Discipline

When synthesising the profile, separate direct user statements from inferred patterns. If evidence is thin, record the field with low confidence or omit optional detail.
